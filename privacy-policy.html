Privacy Policy for Lexi

Last Updated: November 2, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Lexi! This app is developed by SL Play Soft. We are committed to protecting the privacy of children and complying with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email Address: Used for account creation and authentication
  • Name: Used to personalize the child's experience
  • Authentication Provider Info: From Google or Apple Sign-In

Game Activity Data

  • Game progress and scores
  • Completed levels and achievements
  • In-app settings and preferences

3. How We Use Your Information

  • App Functionality: To provide core features and games
  • Account Management: To create and manage accounts
  • Personalization: To customize the learning experience
  • Analytics: To improve our services

We do NOT:

  • Share information with third parties for advertising
  • Display third-party advertisements
  • Sell or rent any personal information

4. Data Security

We use Firebase (Google Cloud) services. All data is:

  • Encrypted in Transit: Protected by HTTPS/TLS
  • Encrypted at Rest: Using AES-256 encryption
  • Securely Stored: On Google Cloud infrastructure

5. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google) - Authentication and database
  • Google Sign-In - Optional authentication
  • Sign in with Apple - Optional authentication
  • Google Play Billing - In-app purchases

6. Parental Rights

As a parent or guardian, you have the right to:

  • Review your child's personal information
  • Request deletion of your child's data
  • Refuse further data collection
  • Delete your child's account at any time

7. Data Retention

We retain information while your account is active. When you delete your account, we permanently delete all personal information within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Lexi is designed for children ages 3-8. We:

  • Do not collect information without parental consent
  • Provide parents with control over their child's information
  • Do not display behavioral advertising to children
